Sweet Arrow Lake County Park Tourism StoryMap

  • Project Description
  • In collaboration with Sweet Arrow Lake County Park and the Schuylkill Conservation District, I created this ArcGIS StoryMap to help educate the general public about all that Schuylkill County's sole county park has to offer. I created maps in ArcMap 10.6 and ArcGIS Online to highlight the walking paths and rentable pavilions that are found within the park.

    This StoryMap is no longer maintained by the County.
