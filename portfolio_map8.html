Moshannon Valley EMS Grant Support Materials

  • Project Description
  • When time permits, I help out other departments at SEDA-COG outside of the Department of Transportation, which houses the GIS Office. In March 2023, I was asked by the Department of Economic Development to create some maps that would supplment their upcoming grant application for Moshannon Valley EMS to build a new training headquarters in Phillipsburg, Pennsylvania (Centre County).

    I created a static, PDF map to attach to their grant application as well as an ArcGIS Instant App, which displayed the same health care and government boundary data. The two maps outline euclidean distance from the potenial training headquarters and additional health care facilities.
