Gamma Theta Upsilon (GTU)

The International Geographical Honor Society
2018-Present

GTU is an international honor society in geography. Gamma Theta Upsilon was founded in 1928 and became a national organization in 1931. Members of GTU have met academic requirements and share a background and interest in geography. GTU chapter activities support geography knowledge and awareness.

Phi Beta Kappa (PBK)

The Nation's Most Prestigious Academic Honor Society
2019-Present

Love of learning is the guide of life. Since our founding in 1776, Phi Beta Kappa has celebrated excellence in the liberal arts and sciences and championed freedom of thought. As America’s most prestigious academic honor society, we are uniquely equipped to advocate for the value and benefits of liberal arts and sciences education.
