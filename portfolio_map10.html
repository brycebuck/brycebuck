Borough of Berwick, PA Community Report Dashboard

  • Project Description
  • In my effort to spread GIS tools to other departments at SEDA-COG, I met with the Department of Community Development in late 2023. During this conversation, it became clear that there was a need to track various community reports (i.e., Official Complaints, Borough Inspections, Zoning Permits) in the Borough of Berwick, a municipality within Columbia County, Pennsylvania that the department oversees.

    I created an ArcGIS Dashboard that the Department of Community Development could update easily without the help of the GIS Office. The user can filter community reports by Low-to-Moderate Income Population (LMI) United States Census Block Groups, which is essential to their work flow, using the selectors at the top of the dashboard. The map is supplemented with the most up-to-date zoning information provided by the Borough that I formatted to fit the displayed Census boundaries.
